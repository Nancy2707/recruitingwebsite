About S&P BSE Sensex Index


S&P BSE SENSEX is a stock index that tracks the performance of the 30 largest and most liquid companies listed on the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE) that operate in the key sectors of the Indian economy. As a market capitalization-weighted index, first compiled in 1986, BSE Index serves as a powerful tool for investors to watch the movements of the Indian stock market.
